Vegetable Stir Fry Recipe

Description

This vegetable stir fry is a quick, colorful, and healthy meal that’s packed with flavor. Loaded with fresh veggies and tossed in a savory sauce, it’s great served over rice or noodles. It’s perfect for a weeknight dinner or meal prep.

  • Broccoli
  • Carrots
  • Bell peppers
  • Snow peas
  • Garlic
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • Cornstarch
  • Ginger
  1. Prepare and chop vegetables
  2. Make the stir fry sauce
  3. Heat oil in a pan
  4. Stir fry vegetables
  5. Add sauce and cook until thickened
